Projects

AgroHub

AgroHub is a robust platform crafted to empower farmers in maximizing their profits through precise expense tracking and insightful market analysis. By offering tools that allow for detailed tracking of all operational costs, AgroHub helps farmers monitor and manage expenses associated with labor, inputs, and equipment. The platform also includes a market analysis feature that provides real-time insights into commodity prices, demand trends, and competitor activity, enabling farmers to make data-driven decisions about when and where to sell their products. With AgroHub, farmers gain a powerful ally in optimizing their profitability and growing their agribusiness sustainably.

Tikiti App

Tikiti App is a versatile ticketing platform that simplifies event management and ticket purchasing for both organizers and attendees. Designed to cater to diverse events, from concerts to workshops, Tikiti allows organizers to create events, manage ticket types (such as early bird, regular, and VIP), and track sales in real time. For attendees, Tikiti provides a user-friendly interface to browse events, book tickets, and access information on event details and locations. Integrated with secure payment systems like M-Pesa, the app also offers unique features like the ability to save funds within the app for future bookings. With a focus on accessibility and seamless transactions, Tikiti App makes event planning and ticket buying straightforward and efficient for users everywhere.

Hospital Management System

The University Clinic Record-Keeping System is a streamlined digital solution designed to simplify the management of medical records for university clinics. Built to replace traditional paper-based systems, this application enables clinic staff to securely store, access, and manage patient records with ease. Key features include patient registration, appointment scheduling, and medical history tracking, which help improve workflow efficiency and enhance patient care. The system is developed with a focus on security and privacy, ensuring that sensitive patient information is protected in compliance with healthcare data standards. By digitizing record-keeping processes, this project reduces administrative overhead and allows medical staff to focus on delivering high-quality care to students.
